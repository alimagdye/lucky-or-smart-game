1. Game Start: The game is played between two players. Each player takes turns to roll the dice.

2. Rolling the Dice: On each turn, a player rolls a single die. The result of the roll is added to the player's current score.

3. Switching Turns: If a player rolls a 1, their current score for that turn is lost, and their turn ends. The next player then takes their turn. If a player rolls any other number, they can choose to roll again or add their current score to their total score.

4. Adding to Total Score: If a player decides to add their current score to their total score, their turn ends, and the next player takes their turn.

5. Winning the Game: The first player to reach or exceed 100 points in their total score wins the game. The winning player is declared, and the game ends.

6. New Game: Players can start a new game at any time by clicking the "New Game" button. A new game resets all scores and starts with a random player.

7. Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • Press P to close the rules and play the game.
  • Press R to roll the dice.
  • Press K to keep the current score and add it to the total score.
  • Press N to start a new game.
